How this was built: crawled the AMA (all four off-road series), ECEA, GNCC, VCHSS, VCORR, ANEC & Hard Enduro, the dual-sport/ADV aggregators, the host clubs, and the off-road parks — following each source to the next until the leads ran out. The calendar above holds every concretely-dated event I could confirm within ~4 hours, June–Nov 2026. The directory holds the rest of the haystack.
  • Drive times are estimates from 21035; the dashed ring is straight-line, not a true drive-time isochrone.
  • "Venue TBA" events (RORR Dual Sport, Fall Brawl, Foggy Mountain) are mapped at an approximate spot until the club posts the location.
  • Dual sports & nationals sell out fast and statuses change daily — confirm on each event/club page before planning. Signup opens on rolling dates.
  • Out past the ring this window (left off the map on purpose): Barbed Wire & Ithaca enduros (NY, ~4.5 hr), Monkey Butt 2-Day (Newark NY, ~5 hr), most VCHSS/VXCS rounds (southern VA), ANEC Burning Rock (Sophia WV, ~5+ hr).
